The commands may have slight grammar errors.

  • Write a similar, paraphrased English command for each one that means the same thing, but sounds more natural.

  • If the sentence does not make sense or is too ambiguous, write "cannot paraphrase".

  • Original: lines that starts with the word and contains lowercase letter

  • Original: word that contains at most two capital letter
  • Word that uses at most two capital letters within them
  • Original: line that contains letter before number
