Instructions

Write a few queries for a calendar app. Below are a few properties of this app:

  • The calendar is divided into "free blocks" and meetings. No two meetings overlap
  • Each block of time has a start time, end time, and a date
  • Each meeting further has a location, a subject, and a list of attendees
  • For this app, we limit the locations to be just "cafe" or "office", subjects to be "alice", "bob", and "eve", and subjects to be "teaching" or "interview"
  • You can query the app to count meetings, create meetings, or remove a block
  • You can filter your queries in various ways (see examples)
  • Your commands must be original and can NOT simply be a copy of an example command

Examples:

  • how many meetings are on jan 6?
  • how many meetings are before 3 pm?
  • which meetings is bob attending?
  • schedule a meeting for 5pm at the cafe on feb 7
